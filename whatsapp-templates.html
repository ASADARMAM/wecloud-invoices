WhatsApp Message Templates

Use these professional templates when sending invoice links to your customers. Choose the appropriate template based on the invoice status.

Note: These templates use placeholders that should be replaced with actual information:

  • {customerName} - The customer's name
  • {invoiceId} - The invoice number
  • {amount} - The invoice amount
  • {dueDate} - The due date (for unpaid invoices)
  • {invoiceLink} - The link to view the invoice
  • {packageName} - The name of the service package
  • {billingPeriod} - The billing period
